About Lowell

Lowell High School is a public school located in San Francisco. Its mascot is the cardinal and its colors are red and white. It has a student body of around 2,700, various sports teams, and numerous student led clubs.

Why I Built This Website

I decided to make a website about the wildlife found in Bodega Bay because marine biology interests me. I recently spent a week there learning about the organisms that live there and the different ecosystems so I decided to share some of that information with others. Even though the information on this website is quite basic, it might inspire curiosity in others to learn more about marine biology and that's always a good thing.
